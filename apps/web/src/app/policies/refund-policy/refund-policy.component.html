Refund policy

Thank you for choosing Cloudy Clip for your trade journaling needs. We are committed to providing you with a high-quality service that meets your expectations. This refund policy outlines our guidelines for refunds and cancellations.

Subscription term and cancellation

  • Subscription term: Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle unless you cancel.
  • Cancellation: To cancel your subscription, please navigate to the Subscription page in your user dashboard. From there, you can easily cancel your subscription at any time. However, please note that our abuse prevention procedures only allow you to cancel your subscription at least 7 days after the initial payment date.
  • Cancellation effective date: Your cancellation will be effective immediately and your access to features will be terminated right away.

Our refund policy

  • Prorated refund: A refund for any unused time on your subscription until the end of your billing cycle will be issued back to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.
  • No refunds for past billing cycles: Refunds will not be issued for past billing cycles, even if you were dissatisfied with the service during those periods.
  • Exceptions: In rare cases, we may consider issuing a full refund if there are significant technical issues that prevent you from using the service effectively.

Additional considerations

  • No free trials: Our service does not offer free trials. If you have subscribed to a paid plan, your subscription will continue until the subscription is canceled.
  • Recurring charges: Please be aware that your subscription will automatically renew unless you cancel. You may adjust your payment method or billing frequency at any time.
